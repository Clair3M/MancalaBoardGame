How to Play Ayoayo

Playing Ayoayo:
The game begins with one player picking a pit and picking up all of the stones within. Moving counter-clockwise, the player
places one stone into each pit until they are out of stones. If the last stone is placed into a pit that already had stones,
pick up those stones and continue.

Rules
1. If you travel all the way around the board while placing stones, do not place stones into the starting pit.
2. If you place the last stone into an empty pit on your side, you capture any pieces in the pit directly across from it.
4. Always place all captured stones into your store.

Winning the Game
The game ends when all 6 pits on one side of the board are empty. The player who still has stones on their side of the
board captures all those stones. The winner is whoever has the most stones in their store.
