How to Play Mancala

Playing Mancala:
The game begins with one player picking a pit and picking up all of the stones within. Moving counter-clockwise, the player places one
stone into each pit until they are out of stones.

Rules
1. If you run into your own store while placing stones, place a stone in the pit. If you run into the other players store, skip it.
2. If you place the last stone into your own pit, you get an extra turn.
3. If you place the last stone into an empty pit on your side, you capture that piece and any pieces in the pit directly across from it.
4. Always place all captured stones into your store.

Winning the Game
The game ends when all 6 pits on one side of the board are empty. The player who still has stones on their side of the board captures all
those stones. The winner is whoever has the most stones in their store.
